Connecting Minds, Empowering Lives: MentalBoost

Free to use application and is open for all mental health service providers and individuals seeking mental health support.

Get Started

Embrace wellness, break stigma, and thrive together. Your mental health matters.

MentalBoost is a mobile application that connects people seeking mental health services with nearby mental health service providers, allowing them to search for providers, select the type of services they require, schedule appointments for online or face-to-face consultations, and find the providers' exact locations.

MentalBoost is a platform for mental health services, the platform is does not offer mental health services directly but just connect the individuals seeking mental health support and mental health service providers. These are also the services offered by MentalBoost to mental health service providers, Mental Health Service providers can easily manage their appointments, Update their offered services anytime, and they will be able to manage and monitor their earnings.
