About MentalBoost

In the bustling halls of Caraga State University, a group of passionate individuals converged with a shared vision to make a lasting impact on mental well-being. Thus began the inspiring journey of MentalBoost.

It all started with a simple conversation, a collective realization that the challenges of modern life often take a toll on mental health. Fueled by a desire to contribute something meaningful, our diverse team, consisting of visionaries like Bryan Ian Ramos, Arabella Mae Matias, and Selwyn Rollorata, embarked on a mission to create a solution that would resonate with individuals seeking solace and support.

The journey was not without its challenges, but each hurdle became an opportunity for growth. As we tested and refined our product, we also nurtured a team culture that values creativity, compassion, and resilience.

Today, MentalBoost stands not just as a startup product but as a testament to our dedication to mental health advocacy. We invite you to join us on this transformative journey a journey where technology meets compassion, and where MentalBoost becomes more than an app; it becomes a companion on your path to a healthier, happier you.

Top 5 Finalists in the Health Technology Idea Pitching

Mental Boost emerges triumphant as one of the Top 5 Finalists in the Health Technology Idea Pitching competition at Caraga State University. The prestigious accolade was achieved during the 6th Caraga Health Research and Development Convention (Health Con '23), a dynamic two-day event dedicated to fostering health awareness among the Caraganons. This gathering serves as a platform to tackle the region's priority health concerns by harnessing the power of science, technology, and innovation. Mental Boost's recognition not only showcases its innovative approach but also underscores its commitment to contributing meaningfully to the region's healthcare landscape.

Regional Pitching Competition on October 16, 2023

MentalBoost participated in the recently-held PSC Regional Pitching Competition on October 16, 2023, conducted via the Zoom platform. Among the array of startups, MentalBoost distinguished itself by emphasizing mental health innovation. Its engagement underscores the significance of addressing mental wellness within the startup ecosystem. The competition's virtual format reflects adaptability to current circumstances while enabling broader participation and innovation. MentalBoost's involvement highlights the evolving landscape of startup competitions and the increasing importance of mental health solutions within entrepreneurial endeavors.
